Who I Am
South Bay raised,
community driven

I'm a bilingual community engagement specialist and program coordinator based in San Diego, CA. I grew up in the South Bay — the same communities I now serve through STEM education, cultural programming, and systems navigation support.

As a first-generation college student, I experienced firsthand what it means to navigate institutions that weren't designed with you in mind. That experience is the engine behind everything I do.

Whether I'm organizing a STEM fair for 500+ attendees, coaching first-gen students through college transitions, or designing bilingual outreach materials — I bring the same commitment: meet people where they are and build something real together.
